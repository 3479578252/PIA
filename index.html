Which tool should I use?

  • →If you're not sure whether your project needs a privacy assessment, start with Do I Need a PIA?
  • →If your project involves health information in a straightforward, lower-risk way, use the Short Privacy Assessment (Health Information) — aligned to the Health Records Act 2001 (Vic).
  • →If your project involves personal information (non-health), use the Short Privacy Assessment (Personal Information) — aligned to the Australian Privacy Principles.
  • →If your project is complex, high-risk, or involves large amounts of sensitive health information, complete the Full Privacy Impact Assessment.
The above does not constitute legal advice, and you should consult a legal representative if you are unsure.
